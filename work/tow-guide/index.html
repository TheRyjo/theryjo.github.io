Towing Guide is a responsive application to view and filter vehicles by their towing capacities.

Towing Guide was conceived, designed, and implemented by Mavice as a means to provide customers streamlined access to towing data present in Ford's physical and digital brochures. It also demonstrated capabilities related to data-driven applications and Adobe Experience Manager (AEM) SPA Editable Components.

Contributions:

  • Back-end data mapping and API
  • Front-end refactoring, fixes, and refinements
  • Dynamic disclosures engine
